The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives
in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to
enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Each of us has tremendous potential inside, but the systems we live in often hold us back. Poverty is not created by the poor, it is created by systems that deny opportunities. Let us build a world where everyone can unleash their potential and live with dignity and self-respect."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
